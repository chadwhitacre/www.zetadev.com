Appendix: Data Structures

  • Array—A sequence of items in contiguous address space.
  • Linked List—A sequence of items not in contiguous address space.
  • Queue—A sequence where the first item "added" is the first item "removed" (FIFO). Think: checkout line.
  • Stack—A sequence where the last item "pushed" is the first item "popped" (LIFO). Think: stack of plates in a spring-loaded cafeteria stack.
  • Deque ["deck"]—A sequence where items can be added/pushed and removed/popped from either end.
